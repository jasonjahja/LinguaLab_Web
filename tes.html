punc·tu·al

/ˈpʌŋktʃuəl/

adjective

happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time.

"He's the sort of person who's always punctual."

Usage Examples

  • Happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time.
  • Arriving or happening at the scheduled time.

Synonyms

On-time Prompt Timely

Origin

Late Middle English: from Latin punctualis from punctum, meaning 'a point.'
